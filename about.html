About Us......

We opened our doors in January 1990. It was driven from a desire to provide quality service that seemed to be lacking at other stores in the area that we used to shop. If you have ever walked into a game store and left feeling like you were a burden to the guy behind the counter, you're not alone. We strive to treat people the same way we wanted to be treated, but never were. We always try and answer your questions and give you the attention you deserve, whether you are buying a action figure or game. We will always do our best to answer any questions you may have, even if you are just looking for advice. We encourage you to stop by and say hello.

We are a family owned business located in the heart of Fresno, California. We supply games for those who are in the hobby of hunting. Whether it is for fun we are here for you. We have been serving the Central Valley for almost 37 years now. Our products action figures to games. We have one of the best product in town, and if we don't have what you want, we wil special order it just for you.

We offer small town service at large discount store prices. Our goal is to develop a lifetime relationship that starts with your first purchase and to provide a knowledgeable sales team eager to help you.
